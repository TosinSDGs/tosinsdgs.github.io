Job Market Paper

Building Energy Performance Standards: Impacts on Building Energy Efficiency and GHG Emissions in Washington, DC

The study evaluates the causal impacts of Washington, DC’s Building Energy Performance Standards (BEPS) using panel data from 2012–2023. The study quantifies the effects of BEPS on energy efficiency and GHG emissions, while exploring differences across ownership types. Results indicate substantial improvements in efficiency and emissions performance, especially among buildings with large pre-policy compliance gaps.
