Job Market Paper

Heterogeneous Effects of Cooling Demand on Household Electricity Consumption using Causal ML Models

Examines how climate-driven cooling demand affects U.S. household electricity consumption using RECS 2020 data and causal machine learning models. Results reveal heterogeneous and distributional impacts, with low-consuming and minority households facing higher burdens — highlighting energy inequality in the face of climate change.

Dissertation

Climate Change, Energy Demand, and Policy Impacts: Analyzing Residential and Building Energy Dynamics in the U.S.

Investigates how climate change intensifies household and building energy demand while assessing the effectiveness of performance-based energy policies. Combines distributional econometrics, causal ML, and panel causal inference to generate evidence for equitable energy transitions.
