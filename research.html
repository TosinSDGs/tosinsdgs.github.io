Research Overview

Job Market Paper

Explores heterogeneous and distributional impacts of climate-driven cooling demand on U.S. household electricity consumption using causal machine learning methods. The study combines the 2020 RECS dataset with modern causal inference techniques to estimate CATE, CQTE, and CSQTE across household types and energy profiles.
