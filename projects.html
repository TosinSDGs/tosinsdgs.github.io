Projects

DC-BEPS-Energy-Policy
Empirical evaluation of Washington, DC’s Building Energy Performance Standards (BEPS) using panel econometrics and fixed-effects models to quantify efficiency and emissions outcomes.
RECS-Quantile-Treatment
Quantile-based causal treatment analysis of U.S. residential electricity consumption using RECS data and causal machine learning estimators to study heterogeneous cooling demand effects.
CwA-Impact-Study
Evaluation of the economic impact of the G20 Compact with Africa (CwA) initiative using causal machine learning to measure investment, growth, and employment effects across African economies.
AFG-Climate-Analysis
Climate shock analysis for Afghanistan combining rainfall, NDVI, and food security indicators to assess drought and flood impacts on agricultural wages and household vulnerability.
NPL-using-Ensemble-Model
Modeling non-performing loans using ensemble machine learning methods to predict credit risk with development finance institution data across multiple economies.
Bank-Ensemble-Project
Cross-country bank-level financial distress modeling using tree-based algorithms and macro-financial indicators for early warning system development.
